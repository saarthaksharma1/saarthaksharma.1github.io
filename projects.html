SMTP Client/Server Project

Python-based SMTP client that demonstrates how email is sent step by step over the network. It creates a TCP connection to a mail server, initiates a secure session using STARTTLS and SSL, and then follows the SMTP protocol by sending commands such as HELO, MAIL FROM, RCPT TO, DATA, and QUIT. The program transmits a simple message, waits for proper server responses after each command, and verifies that the expected reply codes are received. Overall, the goal of the project is to give hands-on experience with socket programming, SSL/TLS encryption, and the SMTP protocol, showing how email transmission works under the hood without relying on built-in mail libraries.

Tools: Python · SSL/TLS · SMTP · Socket · Commands
